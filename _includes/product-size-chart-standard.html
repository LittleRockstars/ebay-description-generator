Width: Measure across garment 2 cm down from armholes. Length: Measure from highest point of shoulder to bottom edge of garment.

Measures are from the manufacturer. The tolerance is +- 1.5cm. 1 inch = 2.54 cm, 1 cm = 0.39 inch.
